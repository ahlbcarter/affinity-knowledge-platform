# Affinity Knowledge Platform

The Affinity Knowledge Platform (AKP) is the structured source of truth for Affinity Home Lending.

AKP is designed to power the website, Google Business Profile, SEO/AEO/GEO, Loan Officer tools, recruiting resources, internal operations, and AI-assisted content generation.

## Core Principle

**Write once. Reuse everywhere.**

## Repository Structure

```text
registry/       Master indexes, IDs, entity types, and relationships
entities/       Source knowledge objects
publishing/     Channel-specific outputs and templates
standards/      Editorial, metadata, and publishing standards
templates/      Reusable content templates
assets/         Logos, images, icons, and design assets
```
