# First-Time Home Buyer Mortgage

## Overview

Buying your first home is an exciting milestone, and Affinity Home Lending is here to help make the mortgage process simple and understandable.

A first-time home buyer mortgage is not one single loan program. It is a personalized financing strategy that helps new buyers compare available options such as Conventional, FHA, VA, USDA, and Down Payment Assistance programs.

## Best For

- First-time home buyers
- Current renters
- Buyers with limited down payment savings
- Buyers who want education and guidance
- Buyers preparing to get pre-approved
