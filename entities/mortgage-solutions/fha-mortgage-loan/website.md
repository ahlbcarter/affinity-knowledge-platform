# FHA Mortgage Loans

## Flexible Financing for Qualified Homebuyers

Whether you're buying your first home or purchasing again, an FHA Mortgage Loan may provide a path to homeownership with flexible qualification guidelines and competitive financing options.

At Affinity Home Lending, we'll help you compare FHA financing with other available mortgage solutions so you can confidently choose the option that best fits your goals.

---

## What Is an FHA Mortgage Loan?

An FHA Mortgage Loan is insured by the Federal Housing Administration (FHA), making it possible for approved lenders to offer financing that may be more accessible for qualified borrowers.

While many people associate FHA loans with first-time homebuyers, the program is available to eligible repeat buyers as well.

---

## FHA Loan Benefits

- Lower down payment options for qualified borrowers
- Flexible credit qualification guidelines
- Eligible gift funds may be used
- Competitive interest rates
- Fixed-rate and adjustable-rate options available
- Assumable mortgage feature on many FHA loans

---

## Is an FHA Loan Right for You?

An FHA Mortgage Loan may be a good fit if you:

- Are purchasing your first home
- Have limited funds available for a down payment
- Need flexible qualification guidelines
- Are buying a primary residence
- Want to compare multiple mortgage solutions

Every borrower's situation is unique. Our team will help you compare FHA financing with Conventional, VA, USDA, Down Payment Assistance, and other available loan programs.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is FHA only for first-time homebuyers?

No. FHA loans are available to eligible first-time and repeat homebuyers.

### Does FHA require mortgage insurance?

Yes. Most FHA loans include both upfront and annual mortgage insurance premiums.

### Can gift funds be used?

Yes. Eligible gift funds may be used according to current FHA guidelines.
